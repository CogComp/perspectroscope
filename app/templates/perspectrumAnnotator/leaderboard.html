Frequently Asked Questions:
  • What is this page? We calculate some measures of quality for the annotations received from different users.
  • How are the quality scores calculated? Agreement score shows how often your annotations are deemed as good labels by others.
  • How is the overall scores calculated? The overall score is calculated as function of (1) number of annotations (2) quality of annotations.
  • How are these going to be used? We use these scores in multiple ways: first, we use these scores to track the quality of the annotations we receive. second, we might reward high-quality annotators. And finally, we can eliminate those annotations that tend to low-quality.
